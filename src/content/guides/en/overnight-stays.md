A day tour shows you San Blas. Staying overnight lets you actually live in it — sunrise and sunset over the same stretch of sand, a sky full of stars with zero light pollution, and an evening around a fire with the Guna family hosting you. Stays run **1 to 3 nights maximum**, booked by cabin *type*, not by a specific named island — most San Blas islands offer near-identical cabins within each category, and the exact island is confirmed closer to your date based on availability.

## The 5 cabin types

- **Hostal Isleño** — shared dormitory, the most budget-friendly way to stay overnight and meet other travelers.
- **Cabaña Privada Clásica** — a private, simple beachfront cabin for couples or small groups who want their own space without the premium price.
- **Cabaña Privada Deluxe** — a step up in comfort from the Classic, still private, still beachfront.
- **Camping Bajo las Estrellas** ("Camping Under the Stars") — for travelers who want the most direct connection to the island, sleeping under an open sky.
- **Cabaña Overwater** — the premium option, built out over the water itself.

Exact pricing (first night, additional nights, deposit) is confirmed by a real person over WhatsApp based on your dates — we don't list it here since rates and availability shift.

## What's included in every cabin type

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from your Panama City accommodation
- ✅ Boat transfers
- ✅ Your chosen cabin
- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands
- ✅ A stop at the Natural Pool
- ✅ A local Guna guide

**Not included:** drinks and snacks, alcoholic beverages, the Guna Yala community tax (paid separately, in cash), snorkel gear, and personal items like towels or soap.

## Sample itinerary (2 days / 1 night)

- **Day 1:** 5:00–5:45 AM pickup in Panama City → 8:30 AM arrival in Carti → 9:30 AM arrival at the island, cabin check-in → 12:30 PM traditional Guna lunch → free afternoon → 6:00 PM traditional Guna dinner.
- **Day 2:** 7:00 AM breakfast → 7:45 AM island tour (Limones Cays, shipwreck snorkel) → 8:30 AM Natural Pool → 9:30 AM return to the island, check-out → 10:00 AM boat back to Carti → arrival in Panama City around 1:00–1:30 PM.

Longer stays (3 or 4 days) follow the same rhythm with an extra free day in the middle — a good time to add the optional 3-island add-on tour.

## Good to know

- **Minimum 3 days' advance booking** required to confirm cabin availability.
- **Late check-out** (until 3:00–3:30 PM instead of 8:30 AM) is available for a small per-person fee.
- Children under 4 stay free; the tool always has the current child rate for each cabin type.
