Isla Pelícano — known by its Guna name **Isla Gorgidub** — earned a very specific kind of fame: it's one of the filming locations used for Netflix's *Money Heist* (*La Casa de Papel*), and fans of the show sometimes plan their whole San Blas trip around seeing it in person. Even without the Netflix connection, it's a striking island — set further out toward the [Dutch Cays](/islands/cayos-holandeses), with clearer water and fewer boats than the islands closer to Carti.

## Why it's a premium stop

Because it sits further from the mainland embarkation point, Isla Pelícano typically appears on VIP or private-tour itineraries rather than standard budget day trips — the extra boat time is worth it for travelers who want a quieter island with excellent snorkeling and, of course, the photo op.

## Good to know

- **Best for:** Money Heist fans, photographers, travelers on a VIP or private tour with more boat time built into the day.
- **Facilities:** minimal — this is a scenic and snorkeling stop, not a lounging island with infrastructure.
- **Distance:** further from Carti than most standard day-tour islands, so it's usually paired with premium or private itineraries that allow more time on the water.
