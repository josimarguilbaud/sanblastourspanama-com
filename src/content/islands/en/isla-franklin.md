San Blas cuisine leans heavily on fresh fish and seafood, and vegan or vegetarian options are limited across most of the archipelago — if you skip the protein at most islands, you simply get a larger portion of sides. Isla Franklin is the one clear exception worth knowing about.

## What makes it different

Isla Franklin is specifically known for offering genuine **vegan meal options** for guests staying overnight — a real point of difference in a region where plant-based travelers usually have to plan around limited choices. If dietary needs are a bigger factor in your trip than the exact island you land on, it's worth mentioning to whoever books your overnight stay.

## Good to know

- **Best for:** vegan and plant-based travelers doing an overnight stay.
- **Booking note:** overnight stays are booked by cabin type, not by a guaranteed named island — if Isla Franklin's food options matter to your trip, mention your dietary needs when you book so it can be factored into planning.
- **Beyond the food:** like other San Blas islands, expect the standard rhythm of an overnight stay — beach time, snorkeling, and a Guna-hosted dinner experience.
