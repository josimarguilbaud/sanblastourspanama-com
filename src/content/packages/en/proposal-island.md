If you're planning a proposal, the two things that actually matter are timing and privacy — which island gets used for a photo doesn't matter nearly as much as making sure the moment itself isn't surrounded by other tour groups.

## How it's built

We work backward from sunset (the most requested moment) and pick an island and cabin combination — often an overwater cabin, since it naturally has private access — where the group traffic from day tours has already cleared out for the day. This isn't a "proposal package" with a staged photographer and props included by default; it's a real overnight stay with the timing and location chosen specifically to give you a genuinely private window.

## If you want more arranged

Some couples want nothing beyond privacy and the view. Others want a photographer, flowers, or a specific setup — if that's you, tell us exactly what you're picturing on WhatsApp and we'll tell you honestly what's realistic to arrange in San Blas versus what isn't, rather than promising a scripted experience we can't actually deliver.
