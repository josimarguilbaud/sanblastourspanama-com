San Blas works well for a group send-off precisely because there isn't a nightlife strip to manage — the day is built around islands, snorkeling, and beach time, and the group stays together instead of splitting off to different bars.

## How it's built

The day-tour portion typically covers multiple islands including Perro Chico, home to San Blas's best-known snorkeling stop — a shallow shipwreck reef right off the beach. For the overnight, we size the cabin to your actual group (this is where headcount matters most: a group of 6 needs a different setup than a group of 14), and build in real beach time rather than a packed, back-to-back schedule.

## Group logistics

Tell us the exact group size, whether everyone wants to stay overnight or just do the day portion, and your dates — we'll confirm real cabin and boat availability for a group your size, since that's the part that varies most from a solo or couple's booking.
