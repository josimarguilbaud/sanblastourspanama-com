Most of San Blas already has weak or no phone signal — this package is for travelers who want to plan around that instead of treating it as a problem to solve.

## How it's built

Up to four days and three nights, without a packed hour-by-hour itinerary. One full day is deliberately left open — no scheduled activity, no boat pickup time, just the island. We lean toward quieter islands like Nugnudub, home to a small Guna community away from the busier tourist stops, and pair it with kayak or paddleboard time you can use whenever you want rather than at an assigned slot.

## Who this is for

If your goal is "see as much of San Blas as possible," a day tour or the shorter overnight stays fit better. This package is specifically for the opposite goal — fewer islands, more time on each one, and stretches with genuinely nothing scheduled. Tell us your available dates and we'll confirm which islands and cabin types work for a longer, slower stay.
