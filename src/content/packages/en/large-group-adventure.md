Shared day tours are built around a fixed group size — once a corporate outing, extended family trip, or large friend group goes past that, splitting across multiple shared tours means your group doesn't actually travel together.

## How it's built

This is transport-first: a private 4x4 from Panama City to the Carti embarkation point and a private boat (or boats, depending on final headcount) rather than seats on a shared shuttle. From there we build a custom island route — the Limones Cays cluster works well for larger groups doing island-hopping without the tightest single-file boat lines — and coordinate one group rate instead of per-person shared-tour pricing.

## What we need to quote this properly

Exact headcount, whether it's a single day or includes an overnight, and your target date — group logistics (how many boats, which cabins can actually fit everyone) depend entirely on real numbers, so this is one of the packages where getting an accurate quote requires a real conversation on WhatsApp rather than a fixed price list.
