A lot of anniversary trips end up as a repeat of the honeymoon itinerary by default, just because that's what the couple already knows. This package starts from a different question: what do you want to do differently this time?

## How it's built

Three days and two nights gives more room than the standard one-night overnight stay — enough for one fully unscheduled day plus a more deliberate pace on the others. We'll ask what you loved (or wish you'd had more time for) on a previous trip, or, if this is your first time in San Blas together, what kind of pace you're after. Cabin choice — deluxe private versus overwater — usually comes down to whether you want a bigger private space on land or direct water access.

## Making it feel different

If there's a specific island, activity, or type of evening you're picturing, tell us on WhatsApp — this package exists specifically so we're not defaulting you into the same standard itinerary every other guest gets.
