Let's be direct about what this is: we don't have a yoga instructor or a wellness program on staff, so this isn't a retreat with scheduled classes. What we can genuinely offer is the setting — a quiet island, unscheduled time, and a cabin base that isn't packed with day-tour groups.

## How it's built

We pick islands known for being low-traffic — Nugnudub and Isla Franklin both see far fewer day-tour visitors than the busiest cays — and build a 2-night stay with no fixed hour-by-hour itinerary, so your mornings and evenings are genuinely open for your own practice on the beach or an open deck. Isla Franklin is also one of the only islands with real vegan meal options, which matters if that's part of your routine.

## Bring what you need

If yoga mats, meditation cushions, or specific dietary needs matter to you, bring them or tell us in advance so we can flag it to the host family — we're arranging the quiet setting and logistics, not running a program, and we'd rather be upfront about that than oversell it.
