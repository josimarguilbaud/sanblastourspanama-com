A standard day tour is built for coverage — as many islands as fit into daylight hours. That's the wrong shape for photography, where the two best windows (first and last light) fall outside a day tour's actual hours entirely.

## How it's built

We anchor this around an overnight stay so you're actually on an island for sunrise and sunset, not on a boat heading back to the mainland. Cayos Holandeses — San Blas's most remote reef cluster — is a strong option for open water and fewer boats in frame, but we'll match the island to what you're shooting: reef and water shots favor different islands than portrait or landscape work.

## Flexibility over checklist

This package trades "most islands visited" for "right light, right island" — which means the itinerary is genuinely built around your dates and what you want to shoot, not a fixed route. Tell us what you're after on WhatsApp and we'll suggest real islands and timing that match.
