Guna Yala has essentially no light pollution, and this is the overnight option built specifically around that fact — sleeping under an open sky, with more stars visible than most travelers have ever seen in one place.

## What to expect

Camping Bajo las Estrellas trades four walls for the most direct possible connection to the island — think a memorable, adventurous night rather than a hotel-style stay. It's a genuinely different experience from our cabin options, and one that consistently gets mentioned as a trip highlight by travelers who choose it.

## What's included

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from Panama City
- ✅ Boat transfers
- ✅ Your camping setup for the night
- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands, plus the Natural Pool
- ✅ Local Guna guide
- ⛔ Alcoholic drinks, snacks, snorkel gear, towels/soap, and the Guna Yala community tax (paid separately)
