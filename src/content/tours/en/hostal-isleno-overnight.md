If a day tour leaves you wanting more time in San Blas but a private cabin isn't in the budget, Hostal Isleño is the answer: a shared dormitory stay that's the most affordable way to wake up on a San Blas island.

## What to expect

Shared dorm-style accommodation means you're sharing the space with other travelers — which, for solo travelers especially, is often a feature rather than a downside. Overnight stays here follow the same rhythm as every San Blas cabin package: beach time, a shared tour to two additional islands, the Natural Pool, and traditional Guna meals with your fellow guests.

## What's included

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from Panama City
- ✅ Boat transfers
- ✅ Shared dormitory accommodation
- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands, plus the Natural Pool
- ✅ Local Guna guide
- ⛔ Alcoholic drinks, snacks, snorkel gear, towels/soap, and the Guna Yala community tax (paid separately)

## Good to know before booking

- **Minimum 3 days' advance booking** to confirm availability.
- **Maximum 3 nights** per stay.
