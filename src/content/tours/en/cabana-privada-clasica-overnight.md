For couples or small groups who want their own private space without paying for the premium overwater experience, Cabaña Privada Clásica is the sweet spot — simple, private, and right on the beach.

## What to expect

This is a private cabin, not shared accommodation — you have your own space for the night, typically with thatched roofing and basic furnishings that keep the focus on where you are rather than how you're housed. Days follow the standard overnight rhythm: arrival and free time on Day 1, a tour to two additional islands and the Natural Pool on Day 2, with traditional Guna dinners each evening.

## What's included

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from Panama City
- ✅ Boat transfers
- ✅ Your private Classic cabin
- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands, plus the Natural Pool
- ✅ Local Guna guide
- ⛔ Alcoholic drinks, snacks, snorkel gear, towels/soap, and the Guna Yala community tax (paid separately)

## Good to know before booking

- **Minimum 3 days' advance booking**, **maximum 3 nights** per stay.
- **Late check-out** available for a small per-person fee if you want extra beach time on your last morning.
