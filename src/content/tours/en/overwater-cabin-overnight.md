This is San Blas's most photographed way to sleep — a cabin built directly out over the water, where the sound of the sea is literally underneath you all night.

## What to expect

The Overwater cabin is our top-tier accommodation: private, built for the setting rather than adapted to it, and the clear choice for travelers who want their San Blas overnight stay to be the centerpiece of the trip rather than an add-on to a day tour. It's a consistent favorite for honeymoons, anniversaries, and anyone prioritizing the view as much as the experience.

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from Panama City
- ✅ Boat transfers

- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands, plus the Natural Pool
