Same private beachfront setting as our Classic cabin, with a genuine step up in comfort — the choice for travelers who want privacy without compromising on how the night actually feels.

## What to expect

Cabaña Privada Deluxe keeps everything that makes a private San Blas cabin appealing — your own space, right on the sand — and improves the finish and comfort level over the Classic option. It's a popular pick for couples marking an occasion, or anyone who wants their overnight stay to feel a little more special without jumping to the full Overwater experience.

## What's included

- ✅ Round-trip 4x4 transport from Panama City
- ✅ Boat transfers
- ✅ Your private Deluxe cabin
- ✅ Full meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- ✅ A tour to 2 additional islands, plus the Natural Pool
- ✅ Local Guna guide
- ⛔ Alcoholic drinks, snacks, snorkel gear, towels/soap, and the Guna Yala community tax (paid separately)
